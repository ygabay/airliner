What is the average cruising speed of an airliner cabin?

This is the average speed at which an airplane travels while it’s in level flight. However, the average speed of an airliner’s cabin — or how quickly it moves from one end of the plane to the other — is much slower. The average speed for a commercial airliner is between 80 and 90 miles per hour.
